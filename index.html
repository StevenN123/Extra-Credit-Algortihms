0/1 Knapsack Problem

Dynamic Programming #2 - Classic Optimization Problem

📋 Problem Description

Given weights and values of n items, put them in a knapsack of capacity W to get the maximum total value. You cannot break items (either take the whole item or leave it).

maximize Σ(vᵢ × xᵢ) subject to Σ(wᵢ × xᵢ) ≤ W, where xᵢ ∈ {0,1}
O(2ⁿ)
Brute Force
O(nW)
DP (2D Table)
O(nW)
Space: O(W)

Example:

Items: (weight, value): (2,3), (3,4), (4,5), (5,6)
Capacity W = 5
Maximum value = 7 (items 1 and 2)
